# Finako AI — Project Vision

> Version: 1.0
>
> Status: Active

---

# Vision

Build an AI-native platform that helps Enterprise Account Managers discover new business opportunities and recommend the right ICT solutions using trusted enterprise knowledge.

Finako AI is not a chatbot.

Finako AI is an enterprise decision support system.

---

# Mission

Build only two world-class capabilities.

1. Enterprise Account Discovery
2. Enterprise Knowledge Platform

Everything else is postponed until these two capabilities are production-ready.

---

# Objectives

Scout AI should be able to:

- Discover new enterprise accounts
- Identify buying signals
- Evaluate opportunity quality
- Explain recommendations
- Recommend relevant IOH products
- Suggest the next best action

Enterprise Knowledge Platform should be able to:

- Store enterprise documents
- Understand document content
- Retrieve trusted information
- Match knowledge with customer needs
- Support Scout AI decisions

---

# Guiding Principles

## Keep It Simple

Choose the simplest architecture that can scale.

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

---

## Truth First

Never invent company facts.

Unknown information must remain unknown.

---

## Evidence First

Every recommendation must include evidence.

Evidence may come from:

- Company website
- Trusted news
- Government publication
- Internal enterprise document

---

## Human Decision

AI recommends.

Humans decide.

---

## Security First

Secrets must never exist inside:

- Source code
- Workflow
- Prompt
- Documentation
- Git history

Secrets must only come from environment variables.

---

# Current Scope

Included

- Scout AI
- Enterprise Knowledge Platform

Excluded

- CRM
- Proposal Generator
- Meeting AI
- Opportunity Management
- Voice AI
- Dashboard

---

# Success Criteria

Scout AI can discover qualified enterprise accounts.

Enterprise Knowledge Platform can answer questions using trusted enterprise documents.

Both capabilities work together with explainable reasoning and evidence.
